About Us

Securing Your Digital Identity

In today's digital landscape, passwords remain the first line of defense against cyber threats. Unfortunately, weak and compromised credentials contribute to over 80% of data breaches worldwide.

Data Breach Insights

  • 86% of breaches involve stolen credentials (Google Cloud, 2023).
  • 74% of breaches stem from human errors & social engineering (Verizon DBIR, 2023).
  • The American Bar Association breach exposed login data of 1.5 million users.
  • Gaming giant Activision suffered a phishing attack that led to unauthorized access.

GenzPass helps to raise awareness about password security and empower users to create stronger passwords, minimizing risks of cyber attacks and check the strength of the existing passwords.
